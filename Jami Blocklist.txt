! === Notes ===
! - Blocking bootstrap + DHT proxies prevents the client from joining or maintaining the OpenDHT network. Default bootstrap: bootstrap.jami.net:4222.
! - Blocking ns.jami.net disables human-readable username resolution to Jami IDs.
! - Blocking turn.jami.net/stun.jami.net breaks NAT traversal for calls and file relays.
! - jami.biz (JAMS) blocks enterprise enrollments/account management.
! - All entries use AdGuard syntax (||domain^) and $important for strict enforcement.
