! === Notes ===
! - AdGuard syntax: `||domain^` blocks root + all subdomains (no `*.` needed).
! - Blocking DHT bootstrap & proxies prevents the client from joining/maintaining the network. (docs confirm use of bootstrap + DHT proxy) 
!   Ref: docs on LAN/DHT & proxy usage, and default bootstrap `bootstrap.jami.net:4222`. 
! - Default TURN is `turn.jami.net` (username/password/realm "ring" in docs) — block to disrupt NAT traversal for calls. 
! - `ns.jami.net` resolves human-readable usernames; blocking breaks username lookups.
! - `jami.biz` (JAMS) is enterprise account manager; included to stop managed-org sign-ins.
